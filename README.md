Objectives
Understand Netflix content distribution over the years
Analyze Movies vs TV Shows trends
Explore popular genres and categories
Study country-wise content production
Analyze ratings and duration patterns

Dataset
The dataset contains information about Netflix titles such as:
Title
Type (Movie/TV Show)
Release Year
Country
Genre (Listed In)
Rating
Duration
Date Added on Netflix

Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn
Jupyter Notebook

Key Analysis Performed
Data Cleaning & Handling Missing Values
Year-wise content growth on Netflix
Movies vs TV Shows comparison
Top producing countries
Most common genres
Rating distribution analysis
Duration analysis (movie length & TV show seasons)

Key Insights
Netflix content increased rapidly after 2015
Movies dominate the platform compared to TV Shows
USA and India are among the top content producers
Drama and Comedy are the most common genres
